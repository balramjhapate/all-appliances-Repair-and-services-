# HomeRepairPro — SEO Master Plan
## Localities + Brands + Page-wise Content Strategy

---

## STRATEGY OVERVIEW

Har SEO page (`/ac-repair-indore`) mein 3 new sections add honge:
1. **Areas We Serve** — us city ke famous localities
2. **All Brands We Repair** — us service ke sare brands
3. **Local Content Paragraph** — localities ka natural mention

**Rule:** Locality names + brand names = long-tail keywords automatically ban jaate hain
Example: "AC repair Vijay Nagar Indore", "LG washing machine repair Kolar Bhopal"
